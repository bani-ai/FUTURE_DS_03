---

## 🔍 Insight 1 — Organic Search Dominates Volume but Isn't the Best Converter
- Organic search generates the most leads (2,296) but has an 11.8% conversion rate — below paid search (12.3%).
- **Action:** Optimize landing pages for organic traffic and add stronger CTAs to close the CR gap.

## 🔍 Insight 2 — Paid Search is the Most Efficient Scalable Channel
- Paid search delivers 12.3% CR — the highest among channels with significant lead volume.
- Every $1 invested here returns the best lead-to-customer ratio.
- **Action:** Increase budget allocation to paid search; run A/B tests on ad copy and landing pages.

## 🔍 Insight 3 — Email Channel is Severely Underperforming
- Email generates 493 leads but converts at only 3.0% — the worst among primary channels.
- This indicates a broken nurture sequence or misaligned audience targeting.
- **Action:** Redesign email workflows with personalisation, strong subject lines, and segmented lists.

## 🔍 Insight 4 — Social Media Has Volume but Low Quality Leads
- Social brings 1,350 leads (second highest volume) but only 5.6% CR.
- Leads from social may be awareness-stage users not ready to convert.
- **Action:** Create dedicated social retargeting campaigns; use bottom-of-funnel content (testimonials, demos).

## 🔍 Insight 5 — Referral Channel is a Hidden Gem
- Referral converts at 8.5% with only 284 leads — strong signal with low investment.
- **Action:** Launch a formal referral program with incentives for existing customers/partners.

## 🔍 Insight 6 — Home Decor & Health/Beauty Are Top Segments
- Home Decor (105), Health & Beauty (93), and Car Accessories (77) dominate won deals.
- **Action:** Develop vertical-specific campaigns and case studies for these segments.

## 🔍 Insight 7 — "Cat" Profile Leads Are Most Likely to Convert
- 48% of won deals come from "Cat" behaviour profiles — methodical, data-driven buyers.
- **Action:** Train SDRs on cat-profile selling strategies; provide detailed specs and comparison materials.

## 🔍 Insight 8 — Conversion Peaked in March–April 2018, Then Declined Sharply
- Peak deals: April 2018 (207 won deals). By October 2018, only 21 deals were closed.
- **Action:** Investigate pipeline health post-April; check if SDR capacity, seasonal demand, or product changes drove the drop.

## 🔍 Insight 9 — Display Advertising Has the Lowest ROI
- Display generates only 118 leads with a 5.1% CR — high cost, low return.
- **Action:** Pause or significantly reduce display spend; reallocate to paid search and SEO.

## 🔍 Insight 10 — 89.5% Drop-off Requires Lead Scoring
- Only ~1 in 10 MQLs becomes a customer — a major revenue leakage point.
- **Action:** Implement a lead scoring model (firmographic + behavioural signals) to prioritise high-intent leads for faster follow-up.

---

## ✅ Top 5 Recommendations (Priority Order)

1. **Increase Paid Search budget** — highest CR, most reliable ROI.
2. **Fix Email nurture campaigns** — 493 wasted leads per cycle.
3. **Build a referral programme** — 8.5% CR with near-zero acquisition cost.
4. **Implement lead scoring** — stop wasting SDR time on low-intent leads.
5. **Focus sales effort on Home Decor & Health/Beauty** — proven high-conversion segments.
